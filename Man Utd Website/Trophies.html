MAN UTD TROPHY PAGE

Manchester United is one of the most successful teams in English Football. It has won 20 League titles, 13 FA Cups, six League Cups and 21 FA Community Shields. United has also won three UEFA Champions Leagues, one UEFA Europa League, one UEFA Cup Winners' Cup, one UEFA Super Cup, one Intercontinental Cup and one FIFA Club World Cup.

Sir Alex Ferguson is one of the most famous managers of the club. He won 38 trophies as manager. This includes 13 Premier League titles, five FA Cups and two UEFA Champions Leagues, between 1986 and 2013. He retired in 2013 and the club have struggled for silverware ever since.
